My name is Massiel La O Trujillo. I'm from Cuba but have lived in the United States for the past five years. I am a freshman Graphic Design student at the University of Central Florida. Since childhood, I have had a passion for art which I used to express myself and cope with the harsh environment around me. I take inspiration from my experiences to create works of art, which vary from watercolour, acrylic, sculpture, writing, and digital design.
